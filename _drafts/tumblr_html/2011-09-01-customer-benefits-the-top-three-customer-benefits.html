Customer Benefits. The top three customer benefits from playing an Innovation Game are: an opportunity to strongly influence current and future products; an opportunity to meet and build relationships with other customers; an opportunity to meet and interact with the product team, including senior executives.
—Innovation Games (Luke Hohmann)
